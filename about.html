Our Story

From a small sewing room to a world of cuddles, learn how CuddleCorner began.

Crafting Joy Since 2015

It all started when our founder, Sarah, couldn't find a soft toy that was both safe for her newborn and cute enough to be a keepsake. So, she made one herself. That first teddy bear, "Bernie," sparked a passion for creating high-quality, safe, and adorable companions for children everywhere.

Today, CuddleCorner is a team of dedicated designers and makers who believe that every child deserves a soft friend to hold onto. We source only the finest, hypoallergenic materials and subject every toy to rigorous safety testing.
